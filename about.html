About

Born to two Fine Arts majors in 1983, he has been exposed to art in nearly all its forms for as long as he can remember. Despite his parents’ incessant nagging to go down the starving artist route, he took up an undergrad Computer Science and later found a way to reconcile his creative skills with his passion for digital media.

Now based in Singapore, Ego works for an internet startup Workday in San Francisco, CA, as part of it's Relevance Engineering UX Team after a recent acquisition of Identified, where he did an Art Director stint.

When he’s not fiddling around with his laptop, he can be found walking aimlessly around town taking photographs, practicing a riff or two on his guitar, or whipping up a mean batch of Japanese curry in the kitchen.

He also has a peculiar affinity for anything involving peanut butter. And bacon. Yes.
